Mardi Gras Rentals

Long time no post! My first version of this didn't save (apparently you can't drag and drop), so this may be shorter than I initially wanted. The company is called "Mardigras.ca", they are a party rental service. They cater to company parties, weddings, and generally to older audiences (no balloons or airbounce machines), their specific products are these: Tents, serving equipment, linens, BBQ & rotisserie, fountains, tables, dinnerware, flatware, chairs, cotton candy & popcorn, food warmers and glassware. Their old logo was clipart of a tent, and their current logo is their name in awkward type with a flag underneath it. They want something simple and modern, but bold, as they want their sign to be clearly visible from the highway (about 50m away). They liked their old tent logo, but they don't want to be known purely as a tent company, so they wanted steer away from it, however, I find a tent really covers all the bases for a party rental company. All these are pretty rough, no color has been added yet. I'll most likely do something lively, rainbow or simply warm colors.
